- Open your dashboard at `http://localhost:5173`
- Look at the **top-right corner** of the page
- You'll see two icons: a moon/sun and a bell

### **Step 2: Click the Moon Icon ğŸŒ™**
- In **light mode**, you'll see a **moon icon**

- Adjusted colors for better contrast

## Troubleshooting

### **Can't find the button?**
- Make sure you're logged in to the dashboard
- Look at the **very top-right** of the page
- It's next to the bell icon (ğŸ””)
- Try refreshing the page (Cmd+R)

### **Button not working?**
- Hard refresh: **Cmd+Shift+R**
- Clear browser cache
- Check browser console for errors (F12)

### **Theme not persisting?**
- Check if localStorage is enabled
- Try a different browser
- Make sure cookies are allowed
